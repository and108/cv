I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Integrative Media at Wilkes University. Before joining Wilkes University, I was a research and teaching assistant at Old Dominion University. I received my Ph.D. and M.S. in Modeling, Simulation and Visulization Engineering from Old Dominion University. My Ph.D. advisor is Dr. Rick McKenzie. I received my B.S. in Electrical Engineering from Virginia Tech.

Research Interest

My research includes virtual/augmented reality on different applications, serious games, and immersive learning. I am also interested in human-computer interaction in general.
I am always looking for motivated students who are interested in working on serious games and VR/AR related projects. If interested, please contact me via e-mail.
